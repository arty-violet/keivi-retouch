About Me

I’m a High End, Beauty, Fashion, Editorial and Commercial Retoucher based in Moscow, working worldwide.

The world of Photography, Photo Editing and Filmmaking opened for me when I was a child. It seems to me very exciting to create something new, beautiful and unique.

I’m also wild about poetry, theatre, travel and coffee. I’m trying to turn all my feelings into art.

I work closely with my clients. It’s a good skill to find something special in every shining soul.

I believe that I can be happy every day of my life growing as an artist and doing things I love. I’m just putting my heart in all of this.

It’s all about inspiration, self-creation and magic inside.

If you’re interested in my services, please feel free to contact me.

~ Ksenya Violet
