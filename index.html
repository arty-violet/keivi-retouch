About Me

Salut! My name is Ksenya and I’m a High End, Beauty, Fashion, Editorial and Commercial Retoucher based in Moscow, working worldwide.

The world of Photography, Photo Editing and Filmmaking opened for me when I was a child. It seems to me very exciting to create something new, beautiful and unique.

I’m just putting my heart in all of this.

It’s all about inspiration, self-creation and magic inside.

If you’re interested in my services, please feel free to contact me.
